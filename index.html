Giraffe

The giraffe (Giraffa) is a genus of African even-toed ungulate mammals, the tallest living terrestrial animals and the largest ruminants. 

    					
  • Family:   	Giraffidae
    • 
					
  • Weight:   	2,628lb(approx)
    • 
					
  • Height:   	14.1-18.7 ft
    • 
					
  • Predator: 	Lions
    •

Fun facts:

  • The Giraffe is the tallest mammal in the world.
  • Baby Giraffes can stand within half an hour and after only 10 hours can actually run alongside their family.
  • Giraffes spend most of their lives standing up; they even sleep and give birth standing up.
  • Giraffes only spend between 10 minutes and two hours asleep per day.
